About Us

We are an initiative aiming to find solutions to the waste problem and minimize its harm to the Syrian environment.

Our Goals

  • Working on utilizing and recycling waste, and the generated energy from it.
  • Working with responsible authorities to develop a comprehensive waste-management plan.
  • Pressuring decision-makers to enact legal regulations for a better environment.
  • Changing collective behaviors to improve waste management through awareness workshops.
  • Ensuring environmental security for all members of society.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote a sustainable future by advancing recyclability and waste reduction through education, innovation, and community engagement. We collaborate with individuals, businesses, and governments to adopt eco-friendly practices, reduce landfill waste, and create a circular economy where resources are reused, repurposed, and recycled efficiently. Furthermore, we support youth engagement by seeking green opportunities for them that enhance environmental quality.

Our Vision

A world where waste is minimized, every material is recyclable, and communities thrive in harmony with nature. We envision a global movement where responsible consumption and recycling become a way of life, ensuring a cleaner, greener planet for future generations.

Code of Conduct

1. Commitment to Sustainability

We prioritize environmental responsibility in all activities, promoting waste reduction, recycling, and circular economy principles.

2. Integrity & Accountability

Act with honesty, transparency, and fairness in all operations. Take responsibility for decisions and their environmental/social impacts.

3. Respect & Inclusivity

Treat all individuals with dignity. Encourage youth participation and provide equal opportunities for growth in green initiatives.

4. Professionalism

Uphold confidentiality and avoid conflicts of interest. Collaborate constructively, respecting diverse perspectives.

5. Safety & Compliance

Follow health/safety protocols and adhere to local and international environmental laws and labor standards.

6. Anti-Discrimination

Maintain a zero-tolerance policy for discrimination, harassment, or exploitation.

7. Community Empowerment

Engage communities with cultural sensitivity. Provide youth with training, mentorship, and fair wages in green jobs.

8. Continuous Improvement

Regularly evaluate and improve programs. Encourage feedback from stakeholders to foster innovation.
