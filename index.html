About Us

We are an initiative aiming to find solutions to the waste problem and minimize its harm to the Syrian environment.

Our Goals

  • Working on utilizing and recycling waste, and the generated energy from it.
  • Working with responsible authorities to develop a comprehensive waste-management plan.
  • Communicating and networking with decision-makers to enact legal regulations for a better environment.
  • Changing collective behaviors to improve waste management through awareness workshops.
  • Ensuring environmental security for all members of society.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote a sustainable future by encouraging recyclability and reducing waste through education, innovation, and community engagement. To collaborate with individuals, businesses, and governments by adopting eco-friendly practices, reducing landfill waste, and creating a circular economy where resources are reused, repurposed, and recycled efficiently. Furthermore, to support youth engagement by creating green opportunities for them that enhance environmental quality.

Our Vision

Reducing waste and ensuring most of it becomes recyclable materials, serving both society and the environment.

Code of Conduct

1. Commitment to Sustainability

We prioritize environmental responsibility in all activities, promoting waste reduction, recycling, and circular economy principles. Every member is expected to lead by example by adopting eco‑friendly practices both professionally and personally.

2. Integrity & Accountability

Act with honesty, transparency, and fairness in all operations, including fund usage and partnerships. Take responsibility for decisions and their environmental and social impacts.

3. Respect & Inclusivity

Treat all individuals — community members, partners, and colleagues — with dignity. Encourage youth participation and provide equal opportunities for growth in green initiatives.

4. Professionalism

Uphold confidentiality when required and avoid conflicts of interest. Collaborate constructively, respecting diverse perspectives in problem‑solving.

5. Safety & Compliance

Follow health and safety protocols in recycling projects and when handling waste. Adhere to local and international environmental laws and labor standards.

6. Anti-Discrimination

Maintain a zero‑tolerance policy for discrimination, harassment, or exploitation. Report misconduct through designated channels without fear of retaliation.

7. Community Empowerment

Engage communities in recyclability programs with cultural sensitivity. Provide youth with training, mentorship, and fair wages in green jobs.

8. Continuous Improvement

Regularly evaluate and improve programs for greater environmental and social impact. Encourage feedback from stakeholders to foster innovation.
